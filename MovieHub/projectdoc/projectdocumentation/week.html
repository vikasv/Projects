About the week releases Page

In this page, I have displayed movies releasing this week. I have showcased info such as movie poster, title, year of release, age qualification, run time of movie, date of release, number of days since the movie was released, ratings, cast and director information. On clicking the poster of the movie it will be directed to IMDB website to show the rating and reviews of the movie. A search functionality is also included in the top right corner of the page.
