About the Home Page

The home page welcomes the user with an attractive carousel of movie posters of the latest and upcoming movies. It also provides an overview of all the information available on the website on the left half of the page and also latest movie news and gossips on the right half of it using RSS feeds. A search functionality is also available on all the pages of the website.
