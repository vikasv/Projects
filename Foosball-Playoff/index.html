Rules:
  • Depending on the number of participants, all the teams will be divided into two groups
  • Each team will play all the others in their own group
  • Team scoring 10 points before their opponents, is declared a winner
  • For all the group stage matches, each win carries 2 points
  • Top 4 teams will proceed to the Semi-finals
  • In case of a tie in proceeding to Semi-finals, number of goals scored/conceded is taken into account
  • Finals will be played for 15 points
  • Venue: All the matches are to be played in #2012, Cityview
